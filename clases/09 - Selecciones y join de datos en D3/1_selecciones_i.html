Method chaining 



            const seleccion = d3.selectAll("rect");
            seleccion.attr("x", 10);
            seleccion.attr("y", 50);
            seleccion.style("fill", "red");

Equivalente a:


            d3.selectAll("rect")
              .attr("x", 10)
              .attr("y", 50)
              .style("fill", "red");
